This release introduces bundled configuration, multi-site support, Microsoft Teams chat integration, feed refresh timestamps, macOS optimizations, and major improvements to security, performance, and overall polish.

## What's New

- **Bundled configuration**: SKIES now comes pre-configured — no setup needed on first launch.
- **Multi-site support**: SKIES can now connect to different sites, not just one.
- **Teams chat**: Open a Microsoft Teams chat directly from user profiles when your organization uses Microsoft sign-in.
- **Feed timestamps**: Feeds now show when they were last refreshed.
- **Search photos**: People can now show profile photos in search results.
- **Open-source licenses**: View the app's dependencies and their licenses in Settings > About.
- **Optimized for macOS**: Avatars, icons, buttons, and content widths now scale properly on larger screens.

## What's Improved

- **Security**: Stronger PIN protection and ephemeral sign-in sessions for better privacy.
- **Performance**: Faster people search and user loading across the app.
- **Rich text**: Links in feed content are easier to tap and display correctly.
- **Read tracking**: Read state now syncs more accurately with timestamps.
- **Profile**: Profile screen loads more reliably with less flicker.
- **Birthdays**: Birthday list adapts to your device size.
- **Accent colors**: Replaced the custom color picker with a wider set of preset accent colors for a more consistent look.
- **Notifications**: Tapping a notification now properly closes any open screen before navigating to the content.
- **Error messages**: Network and server errors now show clearer, more helpful messages.
- **Bookmarks**: Bookmarks screen feels snappier and more responsive.
- **iCloud sync**: Sync status now updates correctly and persists across restarts.
- **Images**: Images in feed items now scale correctly instead of stretching beyond their actual size.
- **Text selection**: Text in feed items can now be selected, and link previews work properly.
- **Pull-to-refresh**: Refreshing feels more reliable with less stale content.
- **Feed refresh**: Posts and activities no longer disappear when refreshing due to incorrect merge ordering.
- **Browser preference**: Web links now open in your chosen browser, either in-app or in the system browser.
- **Sharing**: Shared links work more reliably with your organization's setup.
- **Demo mode**: Switching between demo and real accounts after logging out is more reliable. Read history may reset once after this update.
- **Stability**: Various crash fixes and better recovery from invalid local data.
- **Accessibility**: Improved VoiceOver labels and navigation across the app.
